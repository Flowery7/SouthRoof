Flat Roof Systems

TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen solutions for commercial buildings.


Our commercial flat roofing solutions are engineered for energy efficiency, long-term durability, and watertight performance. TPO is a white, heat-welded membrane that reflects UV rays, lowering energy costs. EPDM is a rubber-based system known for its extreme flexibility and ease of repair. Modified bitumen offers multi-layered strength with excellent impact resistance. All systems are fully adhered or mechanically fastened with reinforced seams and custom flashing details. Ideal for offices, retail, and industrial buildings, our flat roof packages come with thermal insulation options and up to 30-year NDL (No Dollar Limit) warranties.
